Detailed Information on the DHT11 Sensor

DHT11 Temperature and Humidity Sensor

An inexpensive digital sensor for environmental monitoring.

Key Information

  • Type: Digital Temperature and Humidity Sensor
  • Operating Voltage: 3V to 5.5V
  • Temperature Range: 0 to 50°C (32 to 122°F) with ±2°C accuracy
  • Humidity Range: 20-90% RH with ±5% RH accuracy
  • Output Signal: Digital single-bus data
  • Pins: 3-4 pins (VCC, GND, Data, and sometimes NC)
  • Sampling Rate: Max 1 Hz (once per second)

About the DHT11 Sensor

The DHT11 is a basic, ultra-low-cost digital temperature and humidity sensor. It's popular among hobbyists and students for simple environmental monitoring projects with microcontrollers like Arduino. While not as precise as more expensive sensors, it's excellent for applications where general readings are sufficient.

It communicates via a single-wire digital signal, making it relatively simple to interface with an Arduino or other microcontrollers. Libraries are readily available to simplify data reading. Its main advantage is its low cost and ease of use for basic projects.

Common applications include home automation systems, weather stations, and plant monitoring systems where approximate environmental conditions are needed.
